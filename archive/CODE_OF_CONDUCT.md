# Code of Conduct

## Our goal

This project exists to make agentic systems more legible, composable, and trustworthy.

Please contribute in a way that helps keep discussion:
- constructive
- specific
- technically grounded
- respectful

## Expected behavior

- assume good intent
- criticize ideas, not people
- prefer concrete examples over vague disagreement
- keep discussions focused on clarity, composability, safety, and usability

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment
- personal attacks
- bad-faith arguments
- intentionally disruptive behavior
- dismissive or hostile treatment of contributors

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions and contributions to keep the project useful and welcoming.
