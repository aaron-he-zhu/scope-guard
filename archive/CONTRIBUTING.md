# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in Work Contract OS.

## What kinds of contributions are useful

Helpful contributions include:
- better canonical examples
- clearer schema or terminology improvements
- additional adapter patterns
- evaluation scenarios
- anti-patterns from real-world agent workflows
- interoperability suggestions

## What to avoid

Please avoid contributions that:
- turn the project into a full workflow engine
- add vendor-specific complexity too early
- introduce overly heavy process for simple tasks
- weaken the core contract concepts of scope, assumptions, and approval boundaries

## Contribution preference

At this stage, clarity is more valuable than breadth.

Small improvements that make the core idea easier to understand are preferred over large feature expansions.
